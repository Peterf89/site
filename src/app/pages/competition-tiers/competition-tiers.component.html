In February of 2024 Sorare came out with a new Roadmap for the coming period. It consisted of many changes, many of which detailed on other parts of this site, but one of the big ones was the shuffling of leagues into new competition tiers: Champions, Challengers and Contenders.

"The allocation of leagues within each competition is updated once a year and communicated two months before the start of the European season in August. Please note, clubs and league tiers are not expected to change significantly year to year. To determine where each covered league is allocated, we use a combination of Opta Rankings for top-performing clubs in each league, activity on Sorare, and our partnership with clubs and leagues."
